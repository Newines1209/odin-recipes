Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy breakfast treats that are quick and easy to prepare.

They taste great with syrup, honey, fruit, or butter.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Heat a lightly greased pan.
  3. Pour batter onto the pan.
  4. Cook both sides until golden.
  5. Serve with your favorite toppings.
