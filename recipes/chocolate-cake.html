Chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolate cake is a rich and moist dessert enjoyed all over the world.

It is perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or simply satisfying a chocolate craving.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients.
  3. Add the wet ingredients.
  4. Pour into a baking pan.
  5. Bake until done and allow to cool.
